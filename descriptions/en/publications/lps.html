During my M1 internship in the R&D team at the Lyon Scientific Police Laboratory, I had the opportunity to participate in the major research project they were conducting at the time, which culminated in the publication of this article, the first in France in the field of forensic science!
In one line, the paper deals with the creation of a statistical model to predict an individual's biological age from the methylation of specific DNA bases.
I was responsible for the entire statistical modeling from A to Z. I also helped at the bench with biological manipulations and with the writing.
